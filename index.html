speakitt.io

While there are a number of apps for teachers to give corrective feedback in writing classes, in speaking classes the options are practically non-existent. In response, we developed an app that allows students to record conversations on their smartphones, while allowing the teacher to give feedback on mistakes. After listening back to their conversations, students can reflect on and correct mistakes as if they were writing a second draft.

If you are a teacher and are interested in using speakitt in your classroom then shoot me an email: devereuxjj@gmail.com

I originally developed speakitt while taking part in Le Wagon's 9-week full-stack coding bootcamp.
